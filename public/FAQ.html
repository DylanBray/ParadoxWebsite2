FAQ

Minors

Minors are welcome during open hours as long as they are accompanied by a parent or guardian.

Reservations

Please call the restaurant or email info@paradoxpizzabeer.com to make a reservation. Please note we do not take any reservations between 5:30pm and 7:30pm Friday or Saturday.

Private Bookings

We offer private bookings and catering. Please email info@paradoxpizzabeer.com for more info.

Delivery

We do not currently offer delivery. Take-out is available via online or over the phone.

Online Ordering

Our online ordering is currently take-out only. Availability is subject to kitchen wait times. The service may get temporarily turned off during busy hours.

Dietary Restrictions

We do our best to accommodate a variety of dietary needs, including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, and common food allergies whenever possible.

Vegan Cheese

We offer a cashew-based vegan cheese as subsitutes for mozzarella and parmesan.

Gluten-free crust

We offer a rice flour based gluten free crust. Though we cannot guarantee cross-contamination, we do our best to prevent it as follows:

  • A seperate mixer is used for the gluten-free dough.
  • Each pizza is cooked in their own seperate pans and the pans are washed after each use.
  • Seperate cutting boards and knives are used to cut the cooked pizzas.
